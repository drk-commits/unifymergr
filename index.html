About Us

Research That Powers Decisions

Unify Mergr Solutions Pvt Ltd was founded in 2018 with a singular purpose: to provide the private equity, venture capital, and M&A ecosystem with research infrastructure it can trust. We operate as an embedded data partner for investment intelligence platforms, deal sourcing tools, and advisory-focused databases.

Our team of trained research analysts and data specialists deliver structured, source-attributed outputs that integrate seamlessly into client schemas — whether you're building a deal intelligence platform, a deal sourcing tool, or an internal intelligence layer for your fund.

Research Coverage

The Depth Behind Our Data

Our research spans asset classes, geographies, and deal stages — building the depth that makes investment intelligence truly actionable.

PE

Private Equity Research

Buyout transactions, growth equity rounds, fund closes, and portfolio company data — tracked from deal announcement through exit. Coverage spans global PE activity with fund-level and deal-level granularity.

VC

Venture Capital Research

Seed through Series D+ rounds, investor participation, valuations, cap table changes, and follow-on tracking. Emphasis on emerging market coverage where data sparsity creates the greatest intelligence gap.

M&A

Strategic acquisitions, divestitures, mergers, and spin-offs — with deal rationale, advisors, multiples, and post-close data. Structured for both sector-specific and cross-border M&A coverage.
